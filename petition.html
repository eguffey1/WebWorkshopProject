Why You Should Sign

They're man's best friend. The most beloved of pets and possibly the sweetest and kindest of animals is not allowed to be with their owners at public establishments. Many people love their dogs and can’t be apart from them. They want to do everything with them. Laws are currently preventing them from enjoying these special moments with their dogs that they would greatly love and appreciate. There is a poorly conceived view held by some as dogd being a nuisance and a problem when they are allowed at or around these establishments and that view is poorly contrived and misconceived. This is an injustice not only to the owners of the banned dogs but to the dogs themselves who only desire to be with their owners and would be excited at the idea of interacting with the many different people that enter these establishments.

Lets Change This Together!

We’d like to give the owners and the guests/customers of these establishments the option of interacting with dogs there. Our goal is to reach out to as many people as we can and spread awareness not only of the problem that is currently affecting many people and their dogs but to lead them towards an existing solution. Anyone who is as passionate as we are about dogs and that wants dogs to be given the right to enter these establishments with their owners will need to know about a petition that is working to rectify this currently existing problem. We believe dogs have the power to brighten people’s lives and bring happiness to those who are sad and we want dogs to have the opportunity to do that from anywhere not just from within a home. For now, we’re starting with breweries but we’d like to expand the reach of our advocacy to extend beyond just breweries and to cover many other types of establishments as well. Details about the aforementioned petition are below.

It takes only 2 minutes
