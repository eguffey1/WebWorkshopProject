Our Mission

As Pups In Public Advocacy we are aiming to help dog owners in Florida be allowed to bring their dogs back into breweries with them. We understand that dogs and beer just go together so why force them to be separate? By signing the petition, you and 40+ thousands of other loving dogs owners can join our fight in letting our dogs back into breweries across the state of Florida.

What We Are Up Against

In the state of Florida, current legislation prohibits dogs who are not registered service animals. And violations of this law are subject to penalties to a maximum of $1000. Craft breweries and wineries fall under the umbrella of a licensed food establishment and are inspected in the same manner as restaurants. The law permits that animals can be kept on a leash and can be outside of a food establishment in clearly defined areas, but may not enter a food establishment for any purposes unless they are currently registered as a service animal.
